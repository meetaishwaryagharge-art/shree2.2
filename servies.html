Separate Dialysis Unit

Our Dialysis Service Unit is staffed by a team of experienced nephrologists, dialysis nurses, and certified technicians who are experts in providing dialysis treatment and managing renal conditions.

We use state-of-the-art dialysis machines that offer precision and efficiency in the delivery of hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis, ensuring the best possible outcomes for our patients.

Central Oxygen and Suction Monitoring

We prioritize the well-being and comfort of our patients at every stage of their healthcare journey. Our Central Oxygen and Suction Monitoring Service is a critical component of our commitment to providing comprehensive care, ensuring that patients receive the necessary respiratory support and medical suction as required.

It allows monitoring of the status and production parameters of all your installations.

  • Central Oxygen Supply
  • Suction Monitoring
  • Emergency Preparedness

Pacemaker

We are proud to offer advanced Pacemaker Services dedicated to the management and treatment of cardiac rhythm disorders. Our commitment to your heart health is unwavering, and our specialized team of cardiologists and electrophysiologists is equipped with cutting-edge technology to provide comprehensive care for patients in need of pacemaker implantation and followup.

Our experienced cardiologists are skilled in pacemaker implantation procedures, and we offer both single-chamber and dual-chamber pacemakers.

  • Expert Cardiac Team
  • Pacemaker Implantation
  • Comprehensive Evaluation
  • Follow-Up Care

Artificial Respiration Through a Ventilator

We understand that critical medical conditions can lead to respiratory distress, making artificial respiration through a ventilator a vital aspect of our lifesaving care. Our state-of-the-art ventilator services are designed to provide critical respiratory support, ensuring that patients receive the oxygen they need to breathe and recover.

Our hospital is equipped with advanced ventilators that deliver precise and customized respiratory support, adapting to each patient's unique needs.

  • 24/7 Availability
  • Critical Care Integration
  • Specialized Respiratory Teams
