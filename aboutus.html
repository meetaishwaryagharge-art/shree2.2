About Us

Trusted Multi-Specialty Healthcare in Karad

Shree Hospital Karad is a well-established multi-specialty healthcare center dedicated to providing high-quality medical services with compassion and integrity. Located in Rukmini Nagar, Karad, we combine modern medical infrastructure with experienced doctors and trained healthcare professionals to deliver reliable and patient-focused care.

Our hospital is equipped with advanced diagnostic facilities, well-maintained operation theatres, and comfortable patient rooms to ensure safety, hygiene, and effective treatment for every patient.
